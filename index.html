IskoDuler

MAKE THE SMART CHOICE

IskoDuler is the perfect tool to help you efficiently select your classes during pre-enlistment. It shows you the probability of getting each subject so you can correctly prioritize your rankings, and properly select which class to take. Scroll down to learn how to use it.

How to use IskoDuler

Step 1

Drag and drop [IskoDuler] (<-- this) to your bookmarks. If your bookmarks toolbar is not visible follow the instructions in the links below.

[Chrome] [Firefox]

Step 2

Log in to your CRS account and go to the pre-enlistment page. You should already have classes desired.

Step 3

Click on the bookmark and the probability will show as a new column. When you edit your pre-enlisted classes, click on the bookmark again to refresh the probabilities.
